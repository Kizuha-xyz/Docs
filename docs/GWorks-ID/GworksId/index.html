GWorks ID

About GWorks-ID#

GWorks ID is a PHP based auth system for Websites and Apps. You can use it to login users to your Websites or Apps without save their information.

Default URI's#

Authorization Page

https://id.g-works.eu/auth/clientID=(AppID)&redirectURI=(Redirect Link)

User Information

https://id.g-works.eu/v2/userData/?token=(token)

This response with json, that include:

  • Username
  • Email
  • Profile Image
  • Permissionlevel at GWorks

First steps to create an app#

  1. Create an Account here
  1. After you created and verified your account log in to the dashboard
  1. Go to Applications and Click on Let us create an App

  1. Now you have your first app. Click now on the Gear to view or edit your app.
  1. Here you can find now your App-ID. For use add a redirect URI at the form at the end of the Page that links to your websites callback page!

✅ You're ready now. You can try it now. Use for this the Authorization page URL and replace (AppID) with your app ID and (Redirect Lnik) with your Redirect link you added in Step. 5!
